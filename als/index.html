The 18-Month Digital Transformation Roadmap

A phased approach ensures a methodical and sustainable transformation, moving from fixing the foundation to driving growth and finally achieving market leadership through innovation.

Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-3)

Objective: Establish a secure and high-performing digital base.

  • Complete website rebuild (mobile-first).
  • Implement all critical security headers.
  • Deploy Google Workspace for staff.

Phase 2: Growth (Months 4-9)

Objective: Drive online visibility and increase student enrollment.

  • Launch SEO and content marketing campaigns.
  • Revamp social media for community engagement.
  • Run targeted paid ad campaigns for admissions.

Phase 3: Leadership (Months 10-18)

Objective: Innovate to become a digital leader in education.

  • Full Moodle LMS rollout for all students.
  • Leverage Google Cloud for data analytics.
  • Scale the E-CADMEY platform as a new revenue stream.
