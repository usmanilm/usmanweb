18-Month Digital Growth Roadmap

Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-3)

Establish Professional Presence

Launch a professional website with clear service offerings and SEO basics. Create profiles on LinkedIn and key UK therapy directories (e.g., Psychology Today, Counselling Directory).

Phase 2: Authority (Months 4-9)

Build Trust Through Content

Begin a blog targeting key client concerns. Share valuable, non-jargon insights on social media to build an audience and demonstrate expertise. Aim for initial client acquisition through organic channels.

Phase 3: Growth (Months 10-18)

Scale Client Acquisition

Introduce targeted Google Ads for high-intent keywords. Develop a lead magnet (e.g., a free guide) to build an email list. Explore offering paid workshops to diversify revenue streams.
