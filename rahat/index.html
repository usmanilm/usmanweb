The Rahat Group has successfully evolved from its 1947 roots in the bakery business into a formidable real estate powerhouse. Understanding Construction Multipliers

The statement "The construction sector's economic impact is further highlighted by its high multipliers in backward (2.29) and forward (1.80) linkages" means:

  • Backward Linkage (2.29): Every Rs. 1 invested in construction generates Rs. 2.29 of demand in upstream supplier industries (cement, steel, labor, transport).
  • Forward Linkage (1.80): The completed construction project enables Rs. 1.80 of downstream economic activity (real estate services, commercial retail, residential utility).

Takeaway: Real estate development is a massive macroeconomic driver, validating Rahat Group's strategic diversification.
