What Are Your Customers Searching For?

Analysis of search trends in Pakistan reveals high intent for specific consultancy services. "Study Abroad" and "Student Visa" services lead the demand, indicating a strong primary market. "Work Visa" services represent a significant and growing secondary market.

Top Searched Destinations from Pakistan

Students and professionals from Pakistan are primarily targeting developed countries for education and work opportunities. The UK and Canada are leading for students, while Gulf countries and European nations are popular for work visas. Marketing efforts should be geo-targeted to these high-demand regions.
