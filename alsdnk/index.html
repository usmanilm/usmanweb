The immediate priority is to fix the broken foundation. This phase is about risk mitigation and creating a stable platform for growth.

Phase 2: Foundational Growth (6-18 Months)

With a secure and fast website, the focus shifts to building authority and generating qualified leads through targeted content and advertising.
